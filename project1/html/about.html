What does this website provide?

Our mission is simple, we created a page where you could sum up what you're up to. You can link your work to your platforms in a very simple way. Get your own unique link and share it with the world. Get to know me was created with simplicity in mind. We intentionally limit the customization options to keep it simple and nice. You are able to chanage your infomation with the navigation controls listed on your account. You can change it as many times as you want, as long as the link you created has not been taken. Also, keep in mind that whenever you change your personal link, people who had the previous one might not be able to reach your profile anymore.
